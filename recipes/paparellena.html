Papa Rellena

Home

Description

Papa Rellena is one of my favorite dishes made by my beautiful peruvian wife. It has a texture similar to mashed potatoes with a filling of ground beef, hard boiled eggs, and delicious black olives.

This peruvian dish is a easy and savory dish that everyone will enjoy.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Ground Beef
  • Black Olives
  • Hard boiled egg
  • olive oil
  • chopped onions

Steps

  1. boil potatoes
  2. chop onions
  3. mash potatoes
  4. brown ground beef
  5. chop up hard boiled egg
  6. form mashed potato into a bowl shape in hand
  7. fill potatoe bowl with meat and eggs
  8. fry papa rellena slightly
